\section{Mate Probing}
\paragraph{Method}
- Some competent models fail easy mate problems, even mate in 1 puzzles.
- We can probe to see if the model knows when the board is mate in 1.
- We can also probe to see if the model knows the best move.

\paragraph{Evaluation}
- Probe the mate for randomised models.
- Predict the best move (with or without decomposing the move)
- Predict the board move squares
